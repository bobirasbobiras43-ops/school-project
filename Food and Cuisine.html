🍜 Japanese Food 🇯🇵

National foods

Japanese cuisine is known for its diversity and balance of flavors as well as the dishes’ presentation. Sushi is a widely known dish made of fresh raw fish served over rice.

Next is ramen, a flavorful noodle soup with unique broths and toppings that may differ per region. Bento boxes are convenient meals that include rice, meat or fish and vegetables inside of a small container.

Tempura, a quintessential Japanese dish featuring seafood and vegetables covered in a light, airy batter and deep-fried until they are crispy. Dango, one of the most unique Japanese meals, is a small rice sweet dumpling on a bamboo skewer often enjoyed with sweet toppings, it is often associated with seasonal festivals, tsukimi (moon-viewing), and hanami (cherry-blossom viewing), having a cultural background. Okonomiyaki, a savory Japanese pancake, made with shredded cabbage and white flour batter, it is often referred to as the "Japanese pizza" offering endless customization options and different toppings.

Finally, A5 Wagyu beef, the most expensive meat in the world (500-700€/kg) has Japanese origins and is known for its tenderness and rich flavour as well as its nutritional value.

Expensive Restaurants

Sukiyabashi Jiro, located in Ginza, Tokyo, is a world famous sushi restaurant known for its top quality ingredients, it may often require reservartion so as to have a spot saved for you. Tempura Kondo, also located in Ginza, Tokyo, offers high quality tempura and stands out for its exceptional lightness. Reservations might also be required.

A5 Wagyu Beef is often served at luxurious restaurants, which helps customers avoid certain restaurants if they are on a tight budget.

Budget friendly Restaurants

Ichiran Ramen, 7-Eleven Japan and Sushiro are all well known places that have restaurants all over Japan for customers looking for cheap yet tasty alternatives. Ichiran Ramen is known for its quick service and its individual booths, being served ramen through a small bamboo curtain, making it a great experience for solo travelling or if you don’t want to socialize at the moment, ramen there is served within 15 seconds of being plated so as to maintain peak freshness. 7-Eleven offers high-quality bento boxes and tasty snacks. Sushiro features a unique way of serving sushi at low prices through a conveyor belt that passes through each seat and the price is determined by the color and pattern of the plate, if a desired plate is not featured on the conveyor belt customers can place their own orders.

Other Restaurant recommendations

Izakayas, which can be found all over Japan, are informal Japanese bars or taverns that serve alcoholic drinks alongside a variety of small dishes. Being reasonably priced and having a lively atmosphere, they are often used for casual gatherings after work, socializing with friends or even for dates. Yakiniku-ya, found all over Japan, specialize in yakiniku (grilled meat) or Japanese-style barbecue. They are known for their hands-on dining experience where customers can cook thin slices of raw meat and vegetables themselves on a built-in grill in the table. They mainly consist of meat and include dipping sauces.
