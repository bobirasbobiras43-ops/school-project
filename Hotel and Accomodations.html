Hotels and Accommodations in Japan

Luxury Hotel

The Ritz-Carlton Tokyo is a 5 star hotel located in the upscale of the Roppongi District, offering panoramic views of the city. It provides guests luxurious rooms, a spa, a fine dining area and top-tier service. It is one of the highest hotels in Tokyo, giving a breathtaking experience for those at the top floors. It is nearby Tokyo Midtown, a modern complex with shops, restaurants and art spaces. Right next to the hotel is also the Hinokicho Park, perfect form of relaxation or a place for walks and it also features sakura in the spring. Besides the park it also close to other popular Sakura spots like Roppongi Hills Mori Garden. The hotel features a Club Lounge and fine views of the Shinjuku area, the Imperial Palace, or Mount Fuji while dining. Guests are also able to enjoy at most luxury hotels in Tokyo an indoor pool and a floor dedicated to health and wellness. The hotel can also be used for hosting weddings in a private designated area. Free wifi is included.

Budget Hotel

Khaosan Tokyo Kabuki is a 2 star hostel found in Asakusa,Tokyo. It is close to the Senso-ji Temple, one of the oldest and most famous temples in Tokyo, as well as a lively area filled with shops, street food and traditional vibes. Its affordability is great for students and travelers on a budget, however cheap it is, it has clean and simple rooms. It is just moments away from Exit 1 on the Ginza Line and Eixt A5 on the Asakusa Line, ensuring easy access to most popular destinations for tourists.
